Fox

Fox is a common name for many species of carnivorous mammals belinging to the canidae family. Foxes are small to medium-sizes canids (slightly smaller than the medium-sized domastic Dogs). characterized by possessing a long narrow snout, and a bushy tail(or brush).

xMembers of about 37 species are referred to as foxes, of which only 12 species.
